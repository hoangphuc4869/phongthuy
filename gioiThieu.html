Master David Goh

Master David Goh (吴宸翔大师) is the third-generation Feng Shui Master in Singapore at the helm of Imperial Harvest. Following in the footsteps of his father, Feng Shui Master Long De Ren (龙德仁师傅) and his grandfather, Feng Shui Grandmaster Fu Tian Long (伏天龙大师), Master David is a firm believer that with good Feng Shui, you can shape and redesign your destined path to achieve your desired goals and aspirations.

introduction

In the 1930s, Master David Goh’s grandfather, Feng Shui Grandmaster Fu Tian Long (伏天龙大师) inherited the art of Imperial Feng Shui from Feng Shui Masters around Southeast Asia and Taiwan. His Feng Shui practice spanned an impressive 50 years, having advised countless clients over the decades. In 1980, this title was passed down to his son, Feng Shui Master Long De Ren (龙德仁师傅), who continued to serve exclusive pools of clientele, consulting them in their homes and offices.

From a young age, Master David took a keen interest in his father’s practice of Feng Shui and Bazi readings. Having grown up with a deep respect for centuries-old Chinese culture, Master David also holds a great appreciation for the power of Feng Shui in helping others. With the exclusive Feng Shui knowledge and experience imparted to him by the Feng Shui Masters before him, Master David formed the foundation of his authentic approach to Feng Shui. Upon graduation from university, Master David answered his life’s calling and took over the reins of the Feng Shui practice, founding Imperial Harvest.

In the years since, Imperial Harvest has assisted thousands of clients in achieving breakthroughs, rapidly growing to serve a prestigious and international pool of clients. Today, Imperial Harvest continues its commitment to Feng Shui expertise, authentic traditions, and exceptional product quality from its luxurious flagship gallery located at Delfi Orchard #02-07/#02-08 and Imperial Harvest Prestige at #03-24/25 in Singapore.
